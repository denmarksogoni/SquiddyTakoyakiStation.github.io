about us

why choose us?

Squiddy takoyaki station is a small food product business located in Sampaguita St. Pembo Makati City, Philippines. We offer affordable yet deliciously made takoyaki with a variety of different flavors.And We created a business website to promote our product and to for easy online ordering with included delivery.

As a food enthusiast, we surely know that the takoyaki they serve here is authentic and appetizing. Nowadays, people want their food to be healthy therefore in order for them to be satisfied, our veggie-cheese takoyaki filling will be the absolute choice for them.
